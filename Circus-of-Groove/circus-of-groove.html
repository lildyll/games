Overview

Use the whole keyboard to catch the beat! Tame a lion, walk the tightrope, and ride a tricycle through 3 unique takes on the rhythm genre!
This game was made in 2 weeks for the CSULB Spring VGDA Game Jam.

Design Process

The theme for this Game Jam was Circus. 8BitDits immediately thought of the structure of a circus performance and thought it would be a perfect opportunity to create a few mini games. We've always wanted to make a rhythm game together and this seemed like the perfect opportunity.

After chosing a color palette we thought would match the circus vibe, 8BitDits immediately cooked up this lion drawing.

Then, almost at the same time, I sent over my idea for the top-down racing bear. You can see the evolution below.

As these were both completed seperately, the art styles were very different. Not only that, but the perspectives were different too! This already set a foundation that each mini game would focus on different ideas for visuals and gameplay while still maintaining the same cohesive style.

Clown Jumpscare!!

The left clown is the original clown I started with. I was trying very hard to get the angle and proportions right, but I was not loving it. I scrapped it and decided focus on the head before working on the challenging body position. That's what turned into the one on the right which looks a lot more cartooney and fun, perfect for our vibe!

Results

In the showcase meeting at the end of the year, we were awarded 2 awards!
We received the "Best Art Award" from the Judges which was a huge honor! Then, after each club member voted for their favorite game, we were awarded the "People's Choice Award".
