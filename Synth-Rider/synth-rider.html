Overview

Skate your way through this hypnotic rhythm adventure with your incredible tricks! Kick your feet and kick the beat and get a killer high score!
This rhythm game was made in 1 week for the 1 Bit Game Jam.

Design Process

The theme for this 1bit Game Jam was beat. Before even beginning any code, I thought it would be interesting to make a rhythm game starting with the song:

Synth Rider Main Theme

The song kept giving me a visual of a cool skater that could "ride the beat." I began designing the character with inspiration from other 1-bit games:

Originally, there was a section at the top of the screen for timing the jumps and tricks. However, through playtesting, it was clear that players would focus on the bar rather than the trick animations. Therefore it was removed.

As someone who has never skateboarded, I did plenty of research for real and fake tricks. Starting with the kick flip created a perfect foundation for the other tricks.

The tricks got goofier as production continued and there was more time to have fun. Then, it felt neccessary to add fun attack animations for hitting the notes... literally!

Once screenshake, explosions, and hitfreeze were added, it all came together!
