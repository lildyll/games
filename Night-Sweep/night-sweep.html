Overview

You are NOT getting paid enough for this! Blast your way through your shift as a robotic janitor trying to clean up your building. Collect different powers with your trusty vacuum to come out on top in this retro-inspired top down shooter!
This game was made in 1 week for the 2025 BigMode Game Jam.

Design Process

The theme for this Game Jam was Power. We had the idea of a game where color determines your power, making sure to limit ourselves to only 4 colors. This also gave us the idea of keeping it down to a semi 1-bit art style as that brings out a lot of creativity through limitation.

After 8BitDits came up with the idea for a janitor vacuuming up enemies, I had the idea of shooting it back out at enemies. This turned out to be my favorite feature.

The boss fight was a unique challenge. We had the idea for 3 floors, but coming up with 3 unique bosses in a week proved to be challenging. After 8BitDits came up with the idea for a cockroach boss, we thought about how you can never kill one of those things!

This inspired us to make the roach the boss for all 3 floors. Each time it showed up, it would have a new power, changing up the fight just enough to make it fresh.

Then came the cover art. The idea was to have an action shot of the robot holding the vacuum up to the camera.
